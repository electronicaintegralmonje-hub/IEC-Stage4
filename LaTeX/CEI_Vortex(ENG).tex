\section{Pair dynamics: Phase dragging and topological torque}

\begin{equation}

\section{Appendix: Detailed derivation of the exact vortex density profile}
\label{app:exact_profile_appendix}

Starting from the vortex profile equation (equation of motion for the $\phi$ field in the static limit), we multiply both sides by $r f'$:

\[
r f' \left[ f'' + \frac{f'}{r} - \frac{n^2 f}{r^2} + m^2 f (f^2 - 1) \right] = 0.
\]

We rearrange the terms so that the left side is a total derivative:

\[
\frac{d}{dr}\left( r (f')^2 \right) = r \left[ 2 f' f'' + \frac{(f')^2}{r} \right] = 2 r f' f'' + (f')^2.
\]

The potential and centrifugal terms are rewritten as:

\[
r f' \left( -\frac{n^2 f}{r^2} + m^2 f (f^2 - 1) \right) = - \frac{d}{dr}\left( \frac{n^2 f^2}{r} \right) + m^2 r f^2 (f^2 - 1).
\]

Adding both sides, we obtain the exact identity:

\begin{equation}
	\frac{d}{dr}\left( r (f')^2 \right) + m^2 r f^2 (f^2 - 1) = \frac{d}{dr}\left( \frac{n^2 f^2}{r} \right).
\end{equation}

Integrating from $r=0$ to an arbitrary $r$:

\[
\left[ r (f')^2 \right]_0^r + m^2 \int_0^r s f^2 (f^2 - 1)\, ds = \left[ \frac{n^2 f^2}{s} \right]_0^r .
\]

Evaluating the limits:
- At $r=0$: $r (f')^2 \to 0$ (since $f(r) \sim r^{|n|}$ and $f' \sim r^{|n|-1}$, thus $r (f')^2 \sim r^{2|n|-1} \to 0$ for $|n|\geq 1$).
- The term $\frac{n^2 f^2}{r}$ at $r=0$ is finite because $f^2 \sim r^{2|n|}$, so $\frac{f^2}{r} \sim r^{2|n|-1} \to 0$.
- Therefore, the integration constant is zero, leaving:

\[
r (f')^2 + m^2 \int_0^r s f^2 (f^2 - 1)\, ds = \frac{n^2 f^2}{r}.
\]

Now solving for $(f')^2$:

\[
r (f')^2 = \frac{n^2 f^2}{r} - m^2 \int_0^r s f^2 (f^2 - 1)\, ds.
\]

In the core regime ($r \ll r_c$), $f(r) \ll 1$ and the potential term is negligible, thus:

\[
r (f')^2 \approx \frac{n^2 f^2}{r} \quad \Rightarrow \quad (f')^2 \approx \frac{n^2 f^2}{r^2}.
\]

Integrating this approximate differential equation:

\[
\frac{df}{dr} \approx \frac{|n| f}{r} \quad \Rightarrow \quad f(r) \propto r^{|n|},
\]

which is consistent with the regularity condition at the origin. Substituting into $\rho_v \approx \frac{1}{2} \phi_0^2 \frac{n^2 f^2}{r^2}$:

\[
\rho_v(r) \approx \pi n^2 \phi_0^2 \left( \frac{r}{r_c} \right)^{2|n|-2}, \quad r \leq r_c,
\]

where $r_c$ appears as a proportionality constant determined by energy minimization.

In the exterior regime ($r \gg r_c$), $f(r) \approx 1 - e^{-(r/r_c)^2}$ (Gaussian tail ansatz satisfying asymptotic conditions), so:

\[
\rho_v(r) \approx \pi n^2 \phi_0^2 \frac{1}{r^2} \left[1 - e^{-(r/r_c)^2}\right].
\]

Combining both regimes yields the exact analytical profile of Eq.~\eqref{eq:exact_profile}. The core radius $r_c$ is fixed by minimizing the energy per unit length $ E/L = 2\pi \int_0^\infty \rho_v r dr $ with respect to $r_c$, giving Eq.~\eqref{eq:rc_final}.
